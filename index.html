About Poly Auto Repair

Poly Auto Repair is a family-owned business in Brooklyn, NY, led by owner Will. With over a decade of experience, we provide top-tier automotive repair services with a commitment to integrity and precision.

Our certified technicians utilize advanced diagnostic tools and industry-leading techniques to ensure your vehicle performs at its best. We prioritize transparency, offering clear explanations and fair pricing.

At Poly Auto Repair, our mission is to build lasting trust with our clients through exceptional service and a customer-first approach. Your vehicle’s safety and performance are our top priorities.
